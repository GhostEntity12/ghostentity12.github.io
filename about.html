About Me

I'm James Leung, a game designer based in Melbourne, Australia.

I work mostly in Unity, where I have moderate proficiency in C#. I also have some experience in hard surface modelling, which has allowed me to quickly work on solo prototypes.

I usually act as a technical game designer and on UI/UX design and programming, though I've also acted as a level designer and a producer on small teams.
