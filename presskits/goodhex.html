Description

Good Hex is Hard to Find is a game about magic, mishaps and managing rats. Control a swarm of rats as they try to revive their benevolent benefactor, a witch, whom they may or may not have killed.

History

Good Hex is Hard to Find has been in development since March of 2022 as the joint capstone unit of the Games and Interactivity and Software Engineering courses at Swinburne University of Technology.

Features

  • Control your rats!

    Use your magic to control rats into doing all the tasks one might need to do in order to revive a witch!

  • A Magical World!

    Spiel about our wonderful and alive world full of art assets and such.

  • Figure it out!

    You'll need to control the chaos of these rats and stay on your toes to do everything you need to in a day, or night!
