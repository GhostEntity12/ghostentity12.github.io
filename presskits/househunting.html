Description

Out in the wilds there exists a vast area of treacherous desert, soothing plains, and dense forest, as well as a plethora of sentient... FURNITURE?! Yes, furniture! Explore this untamed landscape, hunting down chairs, tables, couches, and even giant stuffed bears, all for the sole purpose of decorating your cozy little cabin. So what are you waiting for?! Grab that crossbow, and get to hunting! Created by small indie studio "One House One Fish" and playtested by Ubisoft, House Hunting is an early build of a game where you head out each day and hunt down real, living furniture in order to decorate the cabin of your dreams! (while listening to some great music).

History

House Hunting was first conceptualized in March of 2023 as a joint effort between the Games and Interactivity and Computer Science students at Swinburne University of Technology as part of the capstone unit. The game was one of two proposed ideas brought to light by one of the designers, the other being a fish fighting game given the name “Fishticuffs”. The two game ideas would lead to the team's name being One House One Fish. After pitching both ideas to the cohort of the unit, it was ultimately decided that we would go with House Hunting as it garnered the most positive feedback, with some unique suggestions and ideas from the audience.

Features

  • Explore A Vast Environment!

    Explore a vast landscape consisting of 4 different biomes, each with their own unique features and furniture!

  • Hunt Sentient... Furniture?!

    Carefully maneuver the environment, utilizing terrain and your weapons to snag sentient chairs, tables, beds, and so much more!

  • Decorate Your Home!

    Head home with your newly acquired furniture and being to decorate your interior to best suit you!

  • Planned Features

    More furniture, an expanded map, QOL improvements, a new inventory system, furniture that hunts you back!

Target Audience

The intended age range of our target demographic lies between the ages of 12 and 26 years old. The game's combination of hunting and decorating elements, along with its light-hearted and creative nature, would appeal to casual gamers who enjoy games that can be played in short sessions or at a relaxed pace.

About One House One Fish

One House One Fish is a team of passionate game developers working on their capstone game development project at Swinburne University of Technology, with a dream of one day making it big in the games industry
